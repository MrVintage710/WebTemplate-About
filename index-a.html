ABOUT

I just finished my second year of college at Montana State University studying both English Writing and English literature. Even while I was younger and still learning how to write, I was fascinated and passionate about the art of language and story. That passion never faded, and I spent years of my freetime working to perfect my craft. In the beginning, I was interested in creative prose. But as I’ve worked my way through high school, college, and a few writing related jobs so far, I’ve also fallen in love with business writing, marketing, blogging, essay writing, and much more.

Even though a lot of my time is dedicated to writing, I’ve also been singing for almost eight years. I’ve always liked public speaking after watching my parents do it my entire life. School has been a pleasure for me- I love to learn. Science, psychology, sociology, politics, religion, music, art, business, and much more are all topics that I enjoy and have at least a minimal basis of understanding in order to write more efficiently.

One of the most important things to know about me is my profound love for people. Outside of my field, what truly brings me happiness is loving and supporting friends, family, and even people I don’t know well. I do my best to be friendly and professional through all modes of communication.
